About Mma

The woman behind the words. A fellow traveler, a fellow healer, and still believing that God's love is strongest in the silence.

Mma is the founder of A Safe Place, a global online community dedicated to honest faith, emotional healing, and restoration without the pressure of religious performance.

Known for her gentle yet piercing voice, Mma writes from the space where deep pain meets profound grace, and where the slow, unglamorous work of becoming whole takes place.

She is not a pastor or theologian, and her focus isn't on fame or marketing. Instead, she is committed to helping people, building community, and sharing honest, heartfelt experiences.

The story behind the devotional

This devotional was not written from a mountain top. It was written from the valley. From the floor. From the space between the last tear and the next one. From the honest moments when she told God she was angry, confused, disappointed, and barely hanging on.

And He stayed. He always stayed.

That is what this book is about—not a God who demands you get better before you come closer, but a God who is already close to the brokenhearted. A safe place when nowhere else feels safe.
