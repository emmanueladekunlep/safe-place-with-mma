Meet the Founder

Chidimma Jane Okenwa (Mma) is the heart behind ASP Foundation. She is a fellow traveler, a fellow healer, and still believes that God's love is strongest in the silence.

Known for her gentle yet piercing voice, Mma writes and speaks from the space where deep pain meets profound grace. She is not a pastor or theologian — she is simply a woman who has experienced God's faithfulness in her darkest moments and wants others to know they are not alone.

"I know you're tired. Not the kind of tired that sleep fixes. The kind that lives in your bones. I know because I've been there."

About ASP Foundation

A Christian charity creating safe spaces where people can find hope, healing, encouragement, and genuine community. You are welcome here.

✨ You are welcome here. Just as you are. ✨

At ASP Foundation, we believe that everyone deserves a place where they can be seen, heard, and valued.

We are a Christian charity committed to creating safe spaces where people can find hope, healing, encouragement, and genuine community. Through faith, compassionate support, meaningful conversations, prayer, outreach, and practical resources, we walk alongside individuals navigating life's challenges.

Whether you're carrying grief, battling loneliness, rebuilding after disappointment, or simply looking for a community where you can be yourself, you are welcome here.

Our mission is to remind every person that they are deeply loved by God, worthy of compassion, and never alone.

"This is more than an organisation.
It's a place to breathe.
A place to belong.
A place to heal."

The heart behind the foundation

ASP Foundation was born from a deep understanding of what it means to struggle, to hurt, and to feel alone. It was created for the ones who have been told they're too much or not enough. For the ones who have been wounded by religion but still long for God.

This foundation exists because we believe that everyone deserves a safe place — a place where they can be real, be heard, and be loved without condition.

Mission

To create Christ-centred spaces where people experience hope, healing, belonging, education, practical support, and authentic community.

Vision

A world where every individual knows they are loved, valued, supported, and never have to face life's challenges alone.

What we stand for

Authenticity

A space where you can be real about struggles, doubts, and questions. No performance. No pretending.

Healing without judgment

Emotional, spiritual, and relational restoration at your own pace. No shame. No rush.

Grace & encouragement

You are not too far gone. Love and restoration are always available. You are never beyond reach.

Community & connection

Sharing experiences so no one has to navigate life's challenges alone. You are not alone.
